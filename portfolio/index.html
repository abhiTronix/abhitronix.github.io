  • MeshFlow Controlled Video Stabilization

    2018.12
    • This was a research project, where I re-implemented a state-of-the-art Video Stabilization method completely from scratch in python using OpenCV & scientific libraries such as NumPy, Scipy etc., and it uses a novel MeshFlow motion model (based on findings of Research Paper: Liu, Shuaicheng & Tan, Ping & Yuan, Lu & Sun, Jian & Zeng, Bing. (2016). MeshFlow is a Minimum Latency Online Video Stabilization). The MeshFlow is a spatial smooth sparse motion field with motion vectors only at the mesh vertexes. The proposed method handles spatially-variant motions robustly and its performance is somewhat compareable to YouTube's Video Stabilization. It offers a great potential for a variety of Computer Vision applications such as security systems, robotics, UAVs, etc.
